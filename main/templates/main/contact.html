Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a safari?

You can book a safari through our online booking system, by email, or by calling us directly. We'll guide you through the process and help you choose the perfect package.

What's the best time to visit?

The best time depends on what you want to see. The Great Migration occurs between July and October, while the dry seasons (June-October and January-February) are generally best for wildlife viewing.

What's included in the safari packages?

Our packages typically include accommodation, meals, game drives, park fees, and professional guides. Specific inclusions vary by package and can be customized to your needs.

Do you offer custom safari packages?

Yes, we specialize in creating customized safari experiences tailored to your preferences, schedule, and budget.
